No middlemen.

an online platform for Customers, Co-operations, Farmers and SME’s to easily and freely connect and trade with each other.

Why Lettuce Grow?

Our mission is to increase the competitiveness of small scale farms and businesses, while promoting co-operatives. We connect producers, distributors and consumers directly on a platform.

  Farmers

By creating a free account on Lettuce Grow you are connecting yourself with not only a network of individual customers, but also allows you to easily find a co-operation near you. It doesn’t matter if you grow tomatoes in your garden or have land you grow on. Everyone can set up an account and sell on our platform!

If you wish to remain independent you are more than welcome to create a shop profile and cater to consumers directly, or connect directly with stores and restaurants looking for suppliers!

  Small Business

Whether you own a corner store or a restaurant, by signing up to our platform, you never have to worry about a supplier running out because you can easily find a new one!

We connect you can directly with farmers in order to stock/cook the freshest local produce. You will also be able to be found through our platform by thousands of customers!

  Private Consumer

Creating a free account on Lettuce Grow will allow you access to a proximity search function to easily find the freshest local produce near you. You can also locate restaurants that use the freshest and highest quality local produce.

You will be able to trace exactly where your produce comes from and when it was harvested to guarantee that you receive the freshest produce.

Along with this you will also be able to find stores, restaurants and farms offering delivery services.

  Co-Operations

The EU has provided €4 billion to increase the competitiveness of small scale farms and increase the amount of co-operatives.

Creating a free account on Lettuce Grow will allow you as a Co-op to easily find and connect with small scale farmers in your region. Your profile will also allow farmers to find you and apply to your Co-op.

You will also be able to communicate with other co-operatives to set market prices and promote fair competition.

  Does or will it cost me anything?

Lettuce Grow will always be free to use, our goal is to raise profits for small scale farmers and foster collaboration between small scale farms and SME’s.

Co-operation, Collaboration and Competitiveness is at the heart of our operation. We will only charge a small percentage on every transaction.

  What additional support do you offer?

Along with connecting you with small scale farms, SME’s and other consumers, we will also guarantee quality and transaction security, as well as top notch customer service.

We can also help you locate EU and government grants for which you are eligible.

We are currently gathering support and interest for our platform. Whilst we do this we are also working on developing the platform, the more support we can gather from you the sooner we can launch the platform.
